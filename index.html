About Us

The story of Coco’s Chocolatiers starts not with chocolate, but oddly enough, with graphic design. Now what does chocolate have to do with graphic design you say? Well, This whole journey began when founder Paul Dorsey was assigned a graphic design coding project to design a website for a chocolate company. This is when a huge lightbulb went off in his head where he realized he could combine his two passions in life, chocolate and design, and become a handcrafted chocolate designer. He would use his design background to decorate the chocolate, and his love for chocolate to taste the product. After hours of studying the art of handcrafting chocolates, Paul realized and perfected the perfect recipe. His concepts were simple, find the highest quality ingredients in the world, and blend them together to make the world’s best chocolate. In 2016 the young chocolate making entrepreneur opened his first company named of course, Coco’c Chocolatiers. Now it is unknown the secrets of where he got the name, but experts agree that it is genius. Today, Coco’s Chocolatiers are changing the world with its chocolate ingenuity providing the worlds finest hand crafted truffles, bars, pastries and much, much more!
